Noodles are a type of pasta that are long, thin, and ribbon-like in shape.

Noodles are a type of pasta that are long, thin, and ribbon-like in shape. They are typically made from unleavened egg dough that is rolled out, cut, stretched, or extruded into long strips. Noodles can be made from a variety of flours, including wheat, buckwheat, rice, potato, corn, bean, soybean, yam, mung-bean starch, and sweet potato.
